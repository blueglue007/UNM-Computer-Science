I’m Ready!

Your work to help Patrick impressed SpongeBob so much that he told everyone about it. It has even left Mr. Krabs speechless.

Mr. Krabs is so impressed, he thinks he could use your skills to help improve his business (he likes money). However, he needs to verify your skills first.

Your Task

Mr. Krabs would like you to demonstrate your skills by writing a highly sophisticated artificial intelligence that perfectly emulates SpongeBob. To do this, you just need to write a Python program that prints out the message “I’m ready!” about 5 times. That should be enough to annoy Mr. Krabs and convince him that the program is just like SpongeBob.

This will involve the following:

  • Obtain your CS guest username and initial password from your lab grader.
  • Change your password to something else (accounts.cs.unm.edu).
  • Connect to the CS department computers.
  • Create a hw directory
  • Inside the hw directory, create a hw2 directory.
  • Inside the hw/hw2 directory, create a hw2.py file.
  • Add the necessary code to it using a text editor of your choice.
  • Demonstrate the program to your TA.

Submission

Deadline

Monday, January 28, 2019 - 3:15PM

Submission Procedure

Assignments are to be submitted during lab sessions or instructor office hours by having your grader or instructor evaluate the correctness of your program.

Grading Procedure

Your lab instructor is expecting to see “I’m ready!” 5 times when running your program with the following command: python3 hw2.py
